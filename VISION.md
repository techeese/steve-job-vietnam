# VISION — Học viện Steve at its most incredible

*The 1-page picture of the dream. The loop mines this for epics; revise it when the owner's taste
sharpens. Not a spec — a north star. (See `DESIGN.md` for settled law, `ROADMAP.md` for the queue.)*

## The one-sentence dream
A **sunny, slightly chaotic little Vietnamese university you love just watching** — Kairosoft-grade
crafted pixel-art and fanfare fused with the bite of an editorial cartoon — that is secretly a
**playable essay** on the real đề Văn question: *làm thế nào để Việt Nam có những "Steve Jobs Việt Nam"?*
You don't get told the answer. You build a school, watch the little people grow up and scatter into
the world as engineers, founders, văn-mẫu clerks, coin sharks, and the rare 🍎 — and you arrive at
**your own** answer.

## The feel (what "incredible" looks like, in order of priority)
1. **A campus that breathes.** Detailed characters with faces and volume doing legible, charming
   things at once; arrivals through the cổng, recess football, the Sống-Ảo khoa filming itself, a
   graduation procession. You want to sit and watch it. **Graphics is the #1 dimension — bar not met.**
2. **The satisfaction of growth.** From an empty lot + a viral essay's seed money to a thronged
   academy — the build-up *is* the pleasure. Every milestone earns fanfare.
3. **Biographies, not scores.** Outcomes span years and switch states (the star who becomes a coin
   scammer two years after graduating). Named students, real-world educators honored by name.
4. **The open question stays open.** No dominant strategy — cram, craft, hustle are each a viable,
   distinct *thesis*. The endgame mirrors the player's own school back as an epilogue, not a verdict.
5. **Satire anchored in the real moment.** The Nuôi Em scandal, the THPT essay prompt, văn mẫu,
   sống ảo, coin culture — topical, dry, never cruel, all fictional.

## The pillars (the systems that deliver the feel)
- **The living campus** — the watchable layer; the soul. Always deepening: more activities, more
  individuality, weather/time-of-day, crowd reactions, festivals.
- **The destiny cascade** — graduation produces *potential*; the world decides destiny via the alumni
  FSM. The 🍎 (a real creator) is rare and earned; the văn-mẫu clerk and coin shark are the traps.
- **The school you shape** — buildings, khoas/majors with synergies, teachers, admissions, funding
  with strings, the pantheon of honored educators. Choices with moral tension.
- **The essay frame** — intro, recurring characters, the epilogue assembled from *your* graduates.

## Dream features (epic-shaped; not yet built — mine these)
- A genuine **art step-change**: bigger 2.5D characters and buildings with real volume/side-walls,
  or relaxing "no asset files" for a richer embedded atlas — clearing the "still ugly" bar for good.
- **Weather, seasons, time-of-day** lighting that makes the campus a place, not a board.
- **Festivals & set-pieces**: Tết, graduation, a viral-scandal day — the campus dresses and reacts.
- **Player-customizable students** (hinted by the owner: "characters are customize").
- **Generative campus-lofi BGM**, state-aware (term / Tết / June ceremony / scandal).
- **A shareable end-card / epilogue** — the player's answer to the đề Văn, made beautiful to share.
- **Deeper khoa life**: rivalries, khoa-gated facilities, a dedicated khoa screen, the trưởng-khoa
  visibly present.

## The bar, in one test
*Would a screenshot of this make a stranger scrolling past want to stop and play?* Until the answer is
an easy yes, graphics and charm keep winning the slot.
