# Judge notes from the design workflow (2026-06-13)

## Judge 1 verdict
CONDITIONAL PASS — strong bones, wrong first slice. The exam set piece (identical văn-mẫu openings side by side), the juice-asymmetry rule (dark gets confetti, virtue gets a quiet nod), scouting's Spark-read flavor lines, and Bác Tâm are genuinely excellent and screenshot-worthy. But the four subsystems describe four different games (four stat systems, three Jobs gates, two tick rates, two incompatible HUD morals), and the loop subsystem's MVP cut guts its own thesis: with one lớp-10 cohort, the June exam fires empty in years 1–2; cram is both unnecessary (balanced preset passes everyone) and unpunished (mood/scandal never bite in 18 minutes); and with alumni gifts, scandals, and visible Uy Tín all deferred, virtue never pays and sin never bills inside the arc — the moral DNA is read in tooltips, not felt in play. As specced, the MVP is a charming screensaver with a preset menu. One structural change (inherit three grades at boot) plus a reconciled single spec sheet turns it into a real game; without those, do not build.

### Top problems
- INCOHERENT SPEC: the subsystems define four different stat systems (KT/TN/ST/CM+Hạt giống vs KT/ST/CG/ĐĐ/CN+Vẹt/Nội Lực vs Tố Chất/Điểm Thi/Thực Học/Rỗng vs the UI's 4 stats), three different Jobs gates (threshold cascade vs multiplicative probability vs trait+gates), two tick rates (1 day=1s vs 2s), cohort sizes 12 vs 6 vs 8–12, and outcome counts 5/7/3. An implementer ships either the union (bookkeeping buries the charm) or a random intersection (skeleton).
- EMPTY SPINE BEATS: MVP starts one cohort at lớp 10, but the June exam resolves per lớp-12 student — the annual fanfare beat fires with ZERO participants at ~minute 5 and ~minute 11 of the 18-minute arc. The game's heartbeat plays to an empty hall twice before paying off once.
- THE DIAL DOESN'T EXIST IN THE MVP: đỗ needs Kiến thức ≥50 and Cân bằng alone yields ~+90 over 3 years, so everyone passes without cramming; cram's NOW payoffs (ranking ladder, grant KPIs) are cut, and its friction (mood −2/mo) takes ~20 months to bite. Cram is simultaneously pointless and costless — the central moral tension reduces to a flavorless preset menu.
- VIRTUE NEVER PAYS, SIN NEVER BILLS, IN-ARC: alumni gifts and scandals are deferred to growth layer 3, Uy Tín is near-invisible for 3 years, Nội Lực is cut. Within the 18-minute slice the player can only READ that thực chất pays later; nothing ever demonstrates it. The tension and students subsystems explicitly demand the opposite (Phốt seeds + alumni ledger in MVP; Charity-Chairman time bomb 'must be in v1') — the design contradicts itself on its most load-bearing element.
- CADENCE AS WRITTEN FREEZES THE SCREEN: auto-pausing bubbles every 15–25s means the 'always-moving screen' — the entire Kairosoft draw — is frozen ~25–35% of playtime; and 12 hand-written events over an 18-minute arc at that rate repeat ~4× each. The bubble layer rots by minute 6. A literal decision every 10–20s is also over-promised: real Kairosoft asks ~1 meaningful choice per 30–90s and fills gaps with watchable progress.
- THE MAP RISKS BEING WALLPAPER: the UI correctly makes walking cosmetic for determinism, but no MVP list includes the sprite tells that make watching informative (toolbox kid fixing props, Spark sky-stare, crowd bubbles, zzz in period 1). If no information lives in bodies, players stop looking at the soul of the game and play it as a menu sim.
- THE WIN IS UNREACHABLE IN THE FIRST RUN: at 7% Spark, ~42% of first runs contain zero Spark students, and ST ≥85 in 3 years is marginal even with one — the 18-minute gacha reveal risks being a full row of grey 'Lương Ổn' cards with no near-miss drama. The screenshot moment is locked out of the demo arc.
- DEAD SUMMERS AND A WASTED BEST-MINUTE: with a single cohort there is no tuyển sinh in years 2–3, leaving two ~60-second dead stretches, and the strongest repeatable minute in the whole design (applicant scouting with Spark-read interview lines) fires exactly once, at minute 0, when the player can't yet read it.
- NO FIRST-3-MINUTES SCRIPT: boot drops the player with 200tr, one room, and no forced opening. The first genuine decision is an unprompted preset pick; the sponsor temptation lands in term 1 but its cost (students rehearsing showcases instead of building) isn't visualized in MVP, making it free money — a temptation with no teeth.
- THE UI's SINGLE DUAL-ENDED METER (-100..100 Thành tích⟷Thực chất) IS MECHANICALLY FALSE AND MISTEACHES THE GAME: Tiếng Tăm and Uy Tín are independent axes (you can be high in both — that's the 'cả hai cao' headline); collapsing them to one slider tells the player it's a zero-sum slider when the whole point is two audiences consuming two different truths.

### Fixes applied in synthesis
- RECONCILE TO ONE SPEC SHEET BEFORE ANY CODE: pick ONE stat set (recommend the students subsystem's 5 stats KT/ST/CG/ĐĐ/CN + visible Vẹt, trimmed to 4 bars + mood on the 390px card), ONE Jobs gate (the legible threshold cascade with the ✓/✗ graduation card — legibility is the satisfaction; kill the hidden probability formula), ONE tick (1 day = 1s), ONE cohort size, ONE outcome list (7). Every other subsystem's numbers get ported onto that skeleton.
- INHERIT THREE GRADES AT BOOT (the founder buys a failing cram school: a lớp 12 of văn-mẫu zombies, a lớp 11, your fresh lớp 10). This single change fixes four problems at once: June exam year 1 has real participants at ~minute 5; the first graduation at ~minute 6 teaches the outcome system with kids you didn't shape; their bad outcomes seed ONE in-arc scandal in year 2–3 (the LATER bill lands inside the MVP); and tuyển sinh fires every summer, so scouting becomes the yearly best-minute it deserves to be. Kairosoft does exactly this — you never start from zero.
- MAKE EXAM SCORE (NOT PASS/FAIL) PAY IN MVP: ship a public ranking ladder versus 3 named fictional rivals (the billboard cram academy 'Học viện iSteve Toàn Cầu', a trường làng, one mid school). Rank drives tuition ceiling and applicant count NOW — this gives cram a real, juicy, visible payoff, restoring the temptation that the đỗ-threshold math currently destroys. Rivals are also free satire content.
- SHIP THE MORAL MIRROR'S MINIMUM PAIR IN MVP: one Phốt-seed mechanic with the live 'Nguy cơ phốt: X%/năm' display (legible risk is the house signature), and one in-arc virtue payoff — in summer of year 2, a quality applicant or teacher applies citing rumor ('nghe đồn trường này dạy thật'), explicitly traceable to a virtuous choice the player made. Virtue must pay at least ONCE, visibly-but-quietly, inside the 18 minutes.
- FIX THE CADENCE CONTRACT: restate as spectacle every 10–20s (motes, sparkle procs, walkers, repairs), CHOICE every 30–60s, management every 2–3 min, fanfare every ~5 min. Micro-bubbles do NOT pause — they hover 8–10s and default to [Lờ đi] if ignored (ignoring becomes a choice, and the screen never freezes). Reserve auto-pause for term/year modals. Grow the MVP deck to ~20–24 events via variants, hard no-repeat within a year.
- BUDGET 3–4 SPRITE TELLS AS MVP NON-NEGOTIABLES: the Spark sky-stare (never explained anywhere — the community-discovery jewel), toolbox-kid free repairs, the Truyền cảm hứng lightbulb chain, and zzz-in-period-1. Spawn every event bubble over the involved student's head. These are what make watching the map ≠ wallpaper; cut a room before cutting these.
- GUARANTEE FIRST-RUN GRADUATION DRAMA: seed one Spark-ish applicant in every first run, tune so a deliberate thực-chất MVP run hits Jobs ~10–20% of the time (it IS reachable in 3 years, just hard), and ship the near-miss line ('Thiếu 6 điểm Sáng Tạo nữa thì… thôi, không nói nữa.') in v1. A first reveal of all-grey cards with no sting kills retention at exactly the moment the game asks for a second run.
- SCRIPT MINUTES 0–3: 0:15 place your first room (placement mode is inherently fun — lead with it), 0:45 first micro-bubble over a named kid, 1:15 hire teacher #2 (pick 1 of 2 contrasting archetypes — cô Văn Mẫu vs thầy Coder Tự Học, the moral fork as a hiring screen), 1:45 pick the giáo án preset, ~2:30 the 80tr sponsor lands while the player is staring at a 200tr balance and a 120tr Phòng máy they want. And RENDER the sponsor's cost: your best students physically rehearsing in the showcase hall, motes stopped, while the Xưởng sits empty.
- KILL THE SINGLE-AXIS HUD METER: two meters as designed in the tension subsystem — Tiếng Tăm big/gold/visibly decaying, Uy Tín as small rumor-text that earns a number only after the first cohort graduates. Keep the juice-asymmetry rule (dark payoffs get confetti and coin sounds; virtue gets a teacher's nod and one nhật-ký line) as a named, enforced rule in the implementer spec — it is the single best 'felt not read' instrument in the whole document.
- RESIZE THE SITTING HONESTLY: the UI subsystem itself estimates ~3,000 lines (2–3× a Nuôi Anh iteration). Plan sitting 1 as the walking skeleton (boot → map → placement → tick → save → gates green, students walking), sitting 2 as exam beat + graduation + the 20-event deck, sitting 3 as scandal/virtue pair + ranking ladder. Mirror dayTick() in an engine.js headless replica from day 1 and sweep the year-1–3 cashflow against the 0.6–1.6× temptation band per the owner's existing toolchain (DESIGN.md rule 4) — the current numbers (cram pointless, mood toothless) would fail that sweep today.

## Judge 2 verdict
Strong shared vision, not yet one game. All five designers independently converged on the right skeleton (annual June exam beat, two-track reputation, dark-pays-now/virtue-pays-later-and-secretly, frozen-offline anti-clicker stance, single-file canvas Kairosoft style) — the premise and moral DNA survived fan-out intact. But the subsystems are written in four mutually untranslatable stat languages, two incompatible win-condition philosophies (deterministic cascade vs probabilistic roll), two different clocks (1s/day vs 2s/day), two different map grids (6x8 vs 15x12), four different curriculum UIs, and five different MVP rosters whose union is 3-4 sittings, not one. There are also three genuine legal-safety slips (VinTao, a recognizable real chip CEO, and verbatim Apple/MacBook/Uber mentions) that violate the owner's no-real-names rule. Nothing here is irreconcilable — every conflict has a clear best pick — but a canonicalization pass is mandatory before any code is written, and the first-sitting MVP must be cut to the intersection, not the union, of the five MVP proposals.

# University revision judges (v2, 2026-06-13)

## v2 Judge 1 verdict
CONDITIONAL PASS — the fun is real but it is sitting next to three spec collisions that will ship a chimera, and one structural pacing bug that puts the game's biggest payoff half outside the playable window. What works: the điểm chuẩn histogram with a draggable cutoff is a genuinely tactile star mechanic and the best new idea in the packet; the alumni sim's big-four beats (garage failure 12 months before the keynote, the arrest at the gate, the returning lecturer, the 20/11 envelope stack) are feelable, screenshot-grade drama; the funding trio is correctly staged for the player's head (endowment passive and grey until it earns its pixel, contracts event-paced); and the pantheon's refusal lines, the 'Không trao' beat, and Bác Tâm reading the 2026 đề at the flagpole are warmth, not homework. What fails: (1) the two most load-bearing screens are specced twice incompatibly — university §3.5 keeps the Steve row in the graduation cascade while alumni §3 removes it, and university §4–5 and funding §1 define two different điểm chuẩn systems with different scales, pool formulas, and quota philosophies; (2) the emergent Steve keynote's tuned median arrival (year 8–11) collides with the 12-year run cap, so roughly half of EARNED keynotes never fire and ~half of virtuous decades end with no fanfare at all; (3) below the big four, the alumni layer is spreadsheet noise by design — 2 generic ticker lines a month for 144 people, a red-dot badge that fires monthly and goes blind, 10–30tr gifts that are rounding error by year 6; (4) the pantheon and funding docs define three scholarship systems, two naming economies, and three endowment states for the same content — Stat Babel II; (5) the June–August modal corridor (đồ án ceremony + 5-prize Lễ Vinh Danh + admissions modal + August BXH/bổ sung) costs 3–4 minutes of non-compressible reading per 6-minute year and turns into a chore by year 6, while years 2–3 of the first session (minutes ~7–18) contain no new kind of moment — the drag valley lands exactly where first-session retention is decided. Every one of these has a cheap, concrete fix; apply the fixes below and this is a build-worthy revision.

### Top problems
- CASCADE CONTRADICTION (worst — it is the game's most important screen): university §3.5 declares the graduation cascade UNCHANGED with 6 outcomes including Steve Jobs VN; alumni §3 removes the Steve row, adds Founder/Xuất Ngoại/Thất Nghiệp rows and the quiet Hạt Táo flag. The owner's revision #3 says alumni wins, but as written an implementer ships a chimera of the graduation beat.
- TWO ĐIỂM CHUẨN SYSTEMS: university §4–5 (histogram modal, cutoff 12.00–30.50 step 0.25, pool N=24+1.6×TT, gauss scores, hard quota dial) vs funding §1 (stepper 14–28 step 0.5, score=9+KT/5+Vẹt/25, pool=12+(4−rank)×3+⌊TT/10⌋, NO quota — everyone above cutoff floods in with crowding penalties).
