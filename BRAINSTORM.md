# Steve Job Vietnam — Brainstorm (2026-06-13)

## The cultural moment (why this game, why now)

On **June 11, 2026**, the đề thi tốt nghiệp THPT môn Ngữ văn asked students, in a 200-word
social-commentary essay (Câu 2, 4,0 điểm): *"Nước Mỹ có Steve Jobs, Mark Zuckerberg, Elon
Musk… Làm thế nào để Việt Nam có những 'Steve Jobs Việt Nam'?"* — paired with an AI-themed
reading passage. It sparked a national debate: Bộ GD-ĐT responded publicly, teachers argued
whether tech billionaires belong in a literature exam, and the internet immediately
nominated a famous phone-company CEO whose birthday fell on exam day.

## The unifying frame (all directions share it)

**The game IS your exam answer.** Cold open in phòng thi: the đề on the desk, 25 minutes,
200 words. You daydream yourself into the founder's life — the game — and every ending
renders as your finished 200-word essay on giấy thi, auto-composed from your actual play,
**graded in red ink** by the unseen Cô Bút Đỏ with a score out of 4,0 and one dry margin
comment. The spiritual successor of Nuôi Anh's newspaper endings. Post-exam debate ticker
as epilogue. Barem rubric as achievements. The 25-minute clock as session frame.

## Owner constraints (locked)

1. Aligns with the đề thi THPT event (above). ✓ frame
2. **NOT a clicker this time — genuinely different gameplay.**
3. Single self-contained index.html, Vietnamese-first, dry satire never cruel,
   all characters obviously fictional, fun first, mobile 390px is a release gate.

## Process

3-phase workflow: cultural research (web) + 5 concept lenses in parallel (12+ concepts) →
3 judges (fun / satire / buildability) → synthesis → re-synthesis with the exam constraint.
Full raw data: `brainstorm-raw.json`. One direction (VĂN NỔ, the clicker-engine heir) was
**eliminated by constraint #2** — its best organ (claims you announce = sentences you write)
was grafted into the survivors.

## Direction A — ⭐ RECOMMENDED: Demo Day Disaster
### «Mạng Hội Trường Hơi Yếu — Câu 2 (4,0 điểm)»
**Genre: real-time plate-spinning comedy (dexterity + push-your-luck). Judges: 35/33/34 — unanimous #1.**

You daydream yourself on the keynote stage, milking genuinely-simulated demo phones for
preorder cash while every unit visibly ticks toward a crash. Only the station broadcast to
the big screen mints money — and broadcasting accelerates its failure clocks. Push or cut,
every 2–3 seconds. When a demo dies in front of the hall, a **5-second cover-up wheel**:
'do wifi hội trường' / 'cắt sang video quay sẵn' / 'do anti seeding phá' / honest 'xin lỗi,
mình làm lại'. **Every excuse you pick is typed verbatim into your live 200-word essay as a
"dẫn chứng"** — you watch your own answer fill with ngụy biện in real time.

- Tension dial: budget split show-vs-engineering, where engineering feeds a stat you
  literally cannot see (virtue is invisible); greed converts into plates you personally must spin.
- Demo stations are real little state machines (camera demo, AI translator that mistranslates
  its own error message, benchmark) — every crash is emergent, fair, screenshot-worthy.
- Endings as grades: 1,25/4,0 "Lạc Đề Tự Tin" ("Em đổ lỗi cho wifi ba lần trong một bài 200
  chữ.") · 3,0 "Văn Mẫu Sân Khấu" · 2,25 "Phúc Khảo Vô Vọng" · secret 4,0 "Tỉnh Giấc" — wake
  at minute 20 and write the truth.
- Cast: Cô Bút Đỏ (the red handwriting), Giám Thị Số 2 (sandal squeaks bleed into the dream),
  Phóng viên Bút Sắt (her notebook is in the same handwriting as the red pen).
- Why it wins: "failure is funnier than success" structurally guarantees fun — the house pays
  out in laughs even on a bad roll. Fresh body, no slider anywhere, solved 390px layout
  (essay strip top, big screen middle, station cards in a thumb row).

## Direction B — Anh Hùng Bàn Phím: Phản Đề Trong Đầu
**Genre: real-time comment-section defense (single-feed arcade).**

You try to write the essay, but your head opens a comment section: every doubt spawns as a
procedurally assembled Vietnamese internet comment ('Steve Jobs Việt Nam? Tỉnh lại đi em').
Three verbs: VĂN MẪU (paste a memorized phrase — instant, but carries detectable tells),
GẠCH XÓA (suppress — it respawns as three angrier doubts: Streisand-as-procrastination-math),
VIẾT PHẢN ĐỀ (slow, costs words and seconds, permanently converts that doubt genre into a
paragraph that defends itself). **Time is the only HP** — nothing hurts you except the
25-minute clock. Secret ending: the final doubt-wave argues itself into your essay while you
rest your hand ("Em là người đầu tiên sáng nay dám cãi lại đề bài. Có dẫn chứng. 4,0.").

- Freshest genre body in the slate; the jokes ARE the enemies (content = writing Vietnamese internet).
- Risk: most content-hungry to stay funny; phrase library grows iteration by iteration.

## Direction C — Kỳ Lân Giấy: Ngáo Điểm
**Genre: push-your-luck card-bluffing roguelite (round-based, calm).**

Mid-exam you daydream onto 'Bể Cá Mập Tỷ Đô', pitching the startup your essay promises.
Buzzword cards (AI, Blockchain, Công Nghệ Lõi, Make in Đại Việt) form self-naming combos and
are **appended verbatim to your essay**, spending the 200-word budget while pumping **Điểm Dự
Kiến** — a literal wobbling bubble of the grade you've told yourself you're getting. Sharks
probe; you bluff (bubble holds, smoke compounds) or nói thật (grade deflates on national
television). Push past the threshold and a shark screams **"NGÁO ĐIỂM!"**. Bà Sáu Quỹ Hưu
Trí invests her real pension — and in wake-cut frames her handwriting matches the lunch-money
note from your bà ngoại in your pencil case.

- Strongest single character in the slate (Bà Sáu); 'ngáo giá' → 'ngáo điểm' is a one-syllable
  pun that makes the whole shark lexicon exam-native.
- Calmer, more strategic pace than A/B; cards fan naturally at 390px.

## Eliminated by the no-clicker rule

**VĂN NỔ — Công Nghệ Lõi™ 200 Chữ** (Nổ slider per sentence on the idle/clicker engine).
Its essay-as-claims organ lives on inside all three survivors.

## Recommendation under all constraints

**Direction A — Demo Day Disaster.** The judges' unanimous pre-constraint winner, and the
no-clicker rule removes its only real rival. Skill-based moment-to-moment play (the owner's
fun-first rule), emergent slapstick that markets itself in screenshots, the exam frame
integrated at the mechanical level (excuses → essay; clock; red ink), and an honest MVP:
one keynote scene + one allocation screen + three station state machines + the cover-up
wheel + the grading renderer, buildable in one sitting, deepenable forever.

## ✅ OWNER DECISION (2026-06-13): Direction E — The Academy

The owner chose a fifth direction of their own: a **Kairosoft-style school management sim**
(Pocket Academy DNA). The founder takes the exam question literally and opens an academy to
manufacture the Steve Jobs Việt Nam: build the school, hire teachers, design curriculum,
train students over in-game YEARS; graduates roll outcomes — the rare true "Steve Jobs Việt
Nam", a scammer (who founds a charity — wink at Nuôi Anh), a coin shiller, a văn-mẫu
champion, an honest engineer… Moral dial carried over: **thành tích vs thực chất** —
achievement theater pays now, real education pays years later in who your graduates become.
The exam event recurs as an annual in-game beat. The four directions above stay banked as
minigame/event raw material (the keynote disaster could be a school showcase event; the
shark pitch could be a sponsorship event).
